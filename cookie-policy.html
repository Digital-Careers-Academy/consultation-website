Introduction

Welcome to Worldview Consultancy Ltd. This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. Cookies are used to improve user experience, enhance website functionality, analyze website traffic, and support marketing initiatives. By using our website, you agree to our use of cookies as outlined in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. These files contain information that helps a website remember user preferences, track usage patterns, and deliver a more seamless and personalized experience.

Cookies can be:

  • Session-based: Deleted after you close your browser.
  • Persistent: Stored for a set period to remember your preferences.
Types of Cookies We Use
1. Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable basic functionalities such as security, network management, and accessibility. Examples include:

  • Session cookies to keep you logged in.
  • Security cookies to protect against fraudulent activities.
2. Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website, such as pages visited most often and any encountered errors. The data collected is anonymous and helps us improve our website’s performance and user experience.

3. Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization. They remember user preferences, such as language settings and font sizes.

4. Analytical & Advertising Cookies

These cookies help us understand visitor interactions with our website. They track website traffic, trends, and engagement, and support targeted advertising.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services such as Google Analytics, social media platforms, and advertising networks.

How to Manage and Delete Cookies

You can adjust your cookie settings in your browser to block or delete cookies. However, disabling certain cookies may impact website functionality. To learn more, visit your browser’s help section.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via website notifications or emails.
